Global Imagine OS Protocol (GIOP) v3.0

The unified, formula-defined imaging protocol. GIOP is the canonical source of truth for every mathematical transform, optical DNA signature, sensor characteristic, display model, and neural rendering method in the professional imaging pipeline.

Why GIOP?

  • Interoperability: ARRI, RED, and Sony cameras speak different color languages. GIOP is the Rosetta Stone.
  • Inconsistency: A grade created in one DI suite renders differently in another. GIOP guarantees visual identity on any reference display.
  • Incomprehension: Artists lack a single verified reference for anamorphic streak wavelengths or inverse log formulas. GIOP provides it.
